How often Should Your Pet See a veterinerian

Age Number of visits Reasons for visits
Birth to 1 Year Every 3-4 weeks until he's 17 weeks old Dogs get shots for rabies,distemper-parvo and shots against kennel cough, influenza and Lyme disease.
Cats will get tested for feline leukemia and feline immunodeficiency.
Adult: 1-7/10 years Yearly checkups The pet will get a head-to-tail physical. Dogs are checked for heartworms and distemper-parvo and rabies booster shots and vaccines against kennel-cough.
Outdoor cats should get feline leukemia vaccines
Senior: 7-10 Years and Older Twice yearly check-ups They will get a full physical check-up and get vaccinations along with test to follow up on any problems.

Expert Tip

To get your pet used to going in a carrier to travel to the vet, keep the carrier out and put your pet's and toys in it
